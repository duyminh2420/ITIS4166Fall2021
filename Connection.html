BANH MY RECIPE

Update Delete

This airy Vietnamese baguette is made with a combination of wheat and rice flours with a thin crispy crust. It is filled with pork, pâté, cured ham and a variety of cold cuts.

Cooking times: 30 minutes

Ingerident:

10 oz. pork tenderloin

6 tbsp. hoisin sauce, divided

2 garlic cloves, grated

2 tbsp. fish sauce

1 medium carrot, thinly sliced

1 medium daikon radish, thinly sliced

1 tsp. kosher salt

2 tbsp. vegetable oil

12-inch baguette, sliced in half lengthwise
